About Us

AGL ASIA PACIFIC SDN BHD is a group of highly specialized, experienced professionals offering industrial products,industrial consumables, Engineered equipment's services and solutions catering to the oil & gas, chemical, steel, cement, petrochemical,power and other heavy Industries. Our strengths also include consulting services in the areas of water resource management and energy conservation. Head-quartered in the capital city of Malaysia our operations spread across Asia Pacific (APAC) region.

Our Vision

To be the company that best understands and satisfies the products, services and needs of our customers.

Our Mission

  • To be a leading provider of specialized engineering services for builders, building owners, developers and our industrial customers.
  • To continually improve our standards of service through clear and responsive communication.
  • To approach each opportunity to establish “personal best” in profession, service and quality.
  • To maintain a work environment that encourages and acknowledges exemplary services.

Our Team

Our Team is a unique combination of technical and management experts. Supported with decades of international experience and backed up by necessary infrastructure in areas of our core competence, we provide solutions on a single source responsibility basis  from inception to implementation.
